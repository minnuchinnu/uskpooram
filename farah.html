WS06

Sketching backwards

- Farah Irani

- Auckland, New Zealand

- Workshop name Wearing Colored lenses: A shimmering perspective

Workshop Description

This workshop explores the fusion of Swift urban sketching techniques with shimmering accents and shapes, using minimal mixed media. Participants will discover how to capture the essence of a location efficiently while experimenting with audacious colors.

Supply List

-Any A4 size coloured paper - minimum 2 sheets ( 150-220 gsm)

-Black micron pen - 2 varying thickness

-Watercolor pencils black and white

-Gouache 3 primary colors

-Gouache any 3 metallic colours

-A suitable travel Palette and brushes. One can consider using an empty plastic Palette with a lid for travel purposes.

-A small folding stool (optional)
